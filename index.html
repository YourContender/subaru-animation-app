Glory to Subaru

Subaru cars are known for their use of a boxer engine layout in most vehicles above 1,500 cc. The lone exception is the BRZ, introduced in 2012 via a partnership with Toyota, which pairs the boxer engine with rear-wheel-drive. Subaru also offers turbocharged versions of their passenger cars, such as the WRX, Legacy and Outback XT, Ascent, and formerly the Legacy GT and Forester XT. Subaru goes off-road with ease and performs well as a sports car

Subaru is everyone's dream
